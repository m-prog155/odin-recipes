hamburger

这是一道美味的汉堡！

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat a grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into equal portions and shape them into patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties on the grill or pan for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or pan until lightly browned.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun, then adding lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, and mustard as desired. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve the hamburgers immediately and enjoy!
