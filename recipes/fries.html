fries

Description

这是一道美味的薯条！

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  2. Rinse the potato strips in cold water to remove excess starch, then pat them dry with a paper towel.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Carefully add the potato strips to the hot oil in batches, avoiding overcrowding. Fry until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
  5. Use a slotted spoon to remove the fries from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  6. Sprinkle the fries with salt while they are still hot. Serve immediately and enjoy!
